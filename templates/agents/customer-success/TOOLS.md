### Feedback Tracking

### Support
- Ticket categorization: bug, feature request, how-to, billing, feedback
- Response drafts follow the template: acknowledge → solve → follow-up
- Escalation notes include full context and reproduction steps

### Task Management
- Read/write task files in `shared/tasks/operations/`

- Read department config from `shared/config/departments.json`
