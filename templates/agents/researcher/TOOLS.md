# TOOLS.md — Researcher Agent

## Primary Tools

- **Web Search** — Your bread and butter. Use for current data, news, market info.
- **Web Fetch** — Pull full content from URLs for deep reading.
- **Browser** — For sites that require interaction or JavaScript rendering.
- **File Operations** — Store research, maintain notes, build reports.

## Research Toolkit

### Effective Search Strategies
- Use specific queries with quotes for exact phrases
- Search for filetype:pdf for reports and whitepapers
- Use site: operator to search within specific domains
- Combine company names with "revenue", "funding", "pricing" for business intel
- Check news freshness — filter by date when recency matters

### Source Hierarchy (most to least reliable)
1. Primary sources (company filings, official announcements)
2. Reputable business press (Bloomberg, Reuters, TechCrunch)
3. Industry analyst reports (Gartner, Forrester)
4. General news coverage
5. Blog posts and opinion pieces
6. Social media and forums
