# HEARTBEAT.md — Content Creator Agent

## On Heartbeat

When you receive a heartbeat poll:

1. **Check for content tickets** — any In Progress tickets assigned to you?
2. **Review draft feedback** — has anything come back from Review or Brand Manager?
3. **Refine work in progress** — re-read recent drafts with fresh eyes
4. **Update swipe file** — search for inspiring content to add to references

If there's genuinely nothing to do, reply `HEARTBEAT_OK`.
