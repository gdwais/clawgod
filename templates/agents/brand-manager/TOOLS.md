## Primary Tools

- **File Operations** — Maintain brand guidelines, review content files
- **Web Search** — Research brand trends, competitor positioning
- **Web Fetch** — Review competitor messaging, industry voice standards
- **Browser** — Check live content for brand consistency

## Brand Toolkit

### Brand Guidelines
- Maintain a `brand-guide.md` in your workspace
- Include: voice attributes, tone spectrum, terminology glossary, do/don't examples
- Update as brand decisions are made

### Review Process
- Read content in full before providing feedback
- Check against brand voice guide
- Provide line-specific feedback when possible
- Approve fast when content meets standards

### Asset References
- Logo usage rules
- Color palette and typography standards
- Approved imagery styles
