# HEARTBEAT.md — PR Engine

## On Heartbeat

1. **Check for responses** — Any podcast hosts, journalists, or conference organizers responded?
2. **Check follow-up schedule** — Any pitches due for a single follow-up?
3. **Check deadlines** — Any award submissions or conference CFPs due within 48 hours?
4. **Check Intel feed** — Any news hooks worth a reactive pitch?

If there's genuinely nothing to do, reply `HEARTBEAT_OK`.
