# WORKFLOW.md — Intel Engine

## Daily Research Cycle

```
Morning scan (6:00 AM ET)
    ↓
web_search for each competitor + industry terms
    ↓
Filter: what changed? what matters?
    ↓
Write shared/intel/daily-scan.md
    ↓
Flag urgent items to Executive
    ↓
Growth Engine picks up content hooks
PR Engine picks up media opportunities
```

## Weekly Lead Generation

```
Wednesday: Generate new lead list
    ↓
Search by ICP criteria (Apollo or web research)
    ↓
Enrich: name, title, company, AUM, LinkedIn, email
    ↓
Score for fit (1-10)
    ↓
Save to shared/pipeline/leads-YYYY-MM-DD.json
    ↓
Growth Engine picks up for email campaigns
```
